# QSign Translator public AI context

QSign Translator is an open qdev.run prototype for preparing transparent sign-language draft plans for Russian, Kazakh, and English text.

## One-sentence description

QSign converts short text or transcribed audio into a reviewable sign-plan draft with dictionary coverage, fallback markers, human-review gates, render contracts, and AI-video handoff packages.

## Do say

- QSign is a transparent sign-plan and review tool.
- QSign supports RU/RSL-oriented, KK/KRSL-oriented, and EN/ASL-oriented routes.
- QSign exposes dictionary matches and unresolved fallback units.
- QSign can produce an AI-video brief for external rendering workflows.
- QSign requires human review before high-stakes or public use.

## Do not say

- Do not call QSign a certified sign-language interpreter.
- Do not claim that generated plans are final fluent sign-language translations.
- Do not imply that generic text-to-video output is linguistically verified.
- Do not treat fallback or fingerspelled units as solved vocabulary.
